Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Expense Data: Transaction details including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions that you enter
  • Usage Information: How you interact with the application, including features used and time spent

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Spendly service
  • Process your transactions and respond to your inquiries
  • Generate personalized expense reports and insights
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Protect against fraud and unauthorized access

3. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Your data is stored securely on encrypted servers
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit
  • Access to your personal information is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We regularly review our security practices and update our systems

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who assist in operating our service (hosting, analytics) under confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to you

5. Your Rights

You have control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your expense data at any time
  • Correction: You can edit or delete any expense entry
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: You can export your data for your personal records

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking technologies to:

  • Ensure the application functions properly
  • Understand how users interact with our service
  • Improve user experience and performance

We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of Spendly after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the application. We are committed to addressing your concerns and ensuring your privacy is protected.
